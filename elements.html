Come Dance With Me.

DANCING IS MY PASSION

My name is Sam and I've had a dream to spread positivity dancing. I was inspired by other dancers like Michael Jackson and one day I decided that I would follow my dream full time. For a while I had a fear of people judging me. Focusing on dancing for years, I overcame that fear which made me a stronger person. My dream is to inspire others to follow their passions like Michael Jackson. My hope is to inspire you to take action and not compare yourself to other people, and just be unique in your own way .
